Friendsaver - real friends, closer together.

Your life is exciting and full. But in everyday life you hardly have time for the really important people in your life. Friendsaver helps you and your friends to stay up to date, according to the motto:
Friendship is like a door between people that sometimes creaks or sticks, but is never closed.

YOUR CONTACTS

Based on your contact book, you can always safely keep track of the people who are important to you.

KNOWLEDGE

Add notes and topics to your friends and get to know them better. Never forget one important thing about your friends.

PEOPLE AT HEART

Not all friendships are the same. Friendsaver automatically remembers which people are particularly close to your heart.

REMINDER

Remembering is at the heart of friendship. Regular and customizable reminders prompt you to keep up with your friendships.

SUGGESTIONS

Receive conversation starters tailored to each friend, helping you initiate meaningful discussions and foster closer relationships.

Friendsaver - tailored to your friendships

Social media is mostly about posting more and more. Friedsaver puts your real friendships at the center. Friendship that goes beyond the last post and the last like.

Notes to support your thoughts

What was Maria allergic to again? What was the name of Ali's favourite restaurant and which bands does Sammy idolize? Simply make notes about your friends. That way you'll always have the most important information to hand. Practical - not only when looking for the perfect gift!

Adjusts to your behaviour

Friendships are living and changing relationships. Friendsaver offers you the possibility to adapt the functions to your needs and automatically determines your interaction behavior with your friends. This allows it to support you individually for each contact.

Save and secure

In a society in which the customer is the product and it is no longer possible to trace who knows what about whom, Friendsaver offers a decentralized solution on your cell phone without server-stored data. All data in the app remains on your device.

Your friendship is yours alone and yet technology can help you improve.
